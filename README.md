## üèóÔ∏è The "Blockchain Stuff" Made Simple

### why Aptos?
We use the **Aptos Blockchain** because it is the fastest and safest network for digital commerce. It ensures your transfers are instant and your booking information is immutable (it can't be tampered with).

### What is an NFT here?
Think of our NFTs as **Digital Deeds**. Instead of an email confirmation that's hard to transfer, you hold a token in your wallet. When you sell the token, you are legally and technically transferring the right to that booking.

### Perpetual Royalties
This is our "Secret Sauce." Using **Smart Contracts** (automated digital agreements), we enforce a rule that says: *"Whenever this asset is resold, X% goes back to the original creator."* This incentivizes early adoption and creates a true secondary economy.
